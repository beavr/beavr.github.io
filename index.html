Who We Are

Technology and healthcare combined

Our goal is to use virtual reality to make rehabilitation an engaging and personalized experience. By integrating rehabilitative exercises into VR games, users can enjoy an immersive and exciting new way to reach their goals of recovery. Progress can be tracked and monitored, and the users' experience is customized in relation to their progress. We aim to prevent disabilities from conditions such as stroke by making therapy motivating and accessible.
